Biography

About Me

"Music and performances are the main part of my life"

Hello! My name is Alex Kazak. I am a jazz/classical pianist, composer, arranger, chart & music track writer. Playing piano since age 7, I have worked with many ensembles across countless genres.

Started in jazz band Five Brothers, Instant Trio, and recorded album "New Birthday" with Post Scriptum Jazz. Soloist of the Presidential Orchestra of the Republic of Belarus.

Over 7 years with cruise lines worldwide — performing with extraordinary musicians, singers, and showmen from every corner of the globe. In 2018 I debuted my own solo show to great success.
